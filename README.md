## üìù Project Overview

This project involves the analysis of a sales dataset for **Ferns and Petals (FNP)**, a retailer specializing in gifts for various occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

The goal of this analysis is to uncover key insights related to sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance. By visualizing this data, the project aims to help the company improve its sales strategy and optimize customer satisfaction.

## üéØ Problem Statement & Business Questions

The analysis focuses on answering specific business questions to evaluate performance:

1. 
**Revenue Analysis:** Identify overall revenue and compare revenue generated across different occasions.


2. 
**Operational Efficiency:** Evaluate the average order delivery time and determine if order quantity impacts delivery speed.


3. 
**Seasonality:** Examine how sales fluctuate across different months of the year.


4. 
**Product Performance:** Identify top revenue-generating products and track the performance of the top 5 products.


5. 
**Customer Insights:** Analyze average customer spending and identify which cities place the highest number of orders.

## üìà Dashboard Insights

### 1. Revenue by Occasions & Categories

* **Top Occasions:** The highest revenue generators are **Anniversary** and **Raksha Bandhan**, followed closely by Valentine's Day.
* **Top Categories:** The "Colors" category generates the highest revenue (approx. ‚Çπ10L), followed significantly by "Soft Toys" and "Raksha Bandhan" specific items.

### 2. Seasonal & Temporal Trends

* **Monthly Trends:** Sales show massive spikes in **February** (likely due to Valentine's Day) and **August** (coinciding with Raksha Bandhan).
* **Hourly Activity:** Order volume gradually increases throughout the day, peaking in the evening between **18:00 and 20:00**.

### 3. Geographic & Product Analysis

* **Top Cities:** **Imphal** and **Dhanbad** are leading in terms of order volume, with meaningful contributions from Haridwar and Bilaspur.
* **Top Products:** The "Magnam Set" and "Dolores Gift" are among the top 5 highest revenue-generating products.
